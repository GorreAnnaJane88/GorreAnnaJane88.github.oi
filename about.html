Writing your story could be the most important thing you ever do

Until a couple of years ago, I was a fiction writer, I didn't understand why people would want to write about real life when they could create something far more exciting in fiction world, creating characters and placing them in whatever situations i crafted for them was liberating,exciting.Becoming a writer happened by accident, I was drowned that time by my own negative perception of life not until I allowed my mind to glide back in time, to re-enter a scene in which I ran away.

the real life stuff? that got the big thumbs down.

That is, until I discovered the healing power of wrting stories.

I know everyone has felt this way.Like you are different,like you were in a different world,living in a different life. Fantasizing,imagining. One day i realized that all of my thoughts,hopes,dreams-could come alive.

I realized ink on page were so much than words.They could create life, create a new universe.They could make peaple happy or sad.

I became a writer to escape the world,Yet i also became a writer to save it.
